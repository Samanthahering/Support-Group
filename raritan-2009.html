Raritan- 2009

Education is Knowledge and Power!

Raritan, NJ -- Saturday, May 2, 2009

An educational program was held at Frelighuysen Park to raise public awareness on depression and on suicide prevention. Thank you to Girl Scout Troop #1069 for helping to make the bows and for their baked goods. To Wegmans and to Scott's Florist for their donations. A very special thank you to our wonderful keynote speakers: Ms. Jessica Santos -- NAMI/IOOV Members: Ms. Ivka Orlik and Ms. Jacqueline Armstrong AFSP/NJ Chapter Chairperson Ms. Peggy Farrell -- Raritan Mayor Ms. JoAnn Liptak -- Raritan Councilman: Mr. Victor Laggini

Raritan, NJ -- Sunday, September 2009

Raising public awareness on suicide prevention by marching on the annual John Basilone parade. Thank you to Bill Wagner, survivor of suicide loss, for his support!!

Educating for a healthy tomorrow! One person, one family, one community at a time!
